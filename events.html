Aviation workshop

Date: November 9th, 2016

 

We decided to organize an aviation workshop for the 6th and 7th graders of our school. The workshop involved both theoretical and practical work. Our core committee taught students about control surfaces and general flight dynamics before moving on to the omniwing - an unconventional paper airplane. The students were also introduced to powered aircraft as they were shown how to add small motors to the planes they made, and then fly those planes.
